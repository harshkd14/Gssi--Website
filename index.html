About Us

Empowering Innovation Through Technology

Gurudev Software is a leading technology solutions provider based in Cuddalore, Tamil Nadu. We specialize in robotics training, software development education, and comprehensive outsourcing services. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional education and industry requirements by providing hands-on, practical training that prepares students and professionals for the future of technology.

With a team of experienced instructors and industry experts, we deliver cutting-edge training programs in robotics, software development, and provide reliable outsourcing solutions for businesses. Our state-of-the-art facility at Durai Plaza is equipped with modern labs and resources to ensure the best learning experience for our students.
